About Me

My interest in massage therapy birthed a few years after obtaining personal training certifications (NASM) where I directed much of my focus on corrective flexibility. Over the years, I've become familiar with the integrity of soft tissue, muscles and their fascia (connective tissue ) and how together their dynamic relationship functions to either promote or prevent optimal movement. From there I took action in further advancing my trade by pursuing a license in soft tissue manipulation in 2013. Since then I've dedicated my specialties to corrective, performance, and maintenance therapeutic massage through a wide variety of clinical modalities.

I genuinely want people to be able to live life to their earnest and fullest potential. Whether playing sports, an instrument or playing with your kids/dogs, etc., freedom in movement is living and I focus my practice and skills on helping individuals achieve functional movement. Whether developing a short term treatment plan or continued maintenance, my goal as a massage therapist is to ensure every person I work with is free to live their life with unrestricted movement!
